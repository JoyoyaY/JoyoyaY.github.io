About us

Joyoya Games: Innovative Game Developer Focused on the Russian-speaking Market

Joyoya Games is a dynamic startup game company dedicated to creating fun and engaging gaming experiences for players worldwide, with a special focus on the Russian-speaking market. Our core business is game development, specializing in:

Client Games (Mobile): Developing high-quality, downloadable game applications. HTML5 Games (Web/Instant Play): Creating lightweight, browser-based games playable instantly.

Core Market & Platforms:

We are currently deeply rooted in the Russian-speaking market, primarily distributing and operating our games on the following platforms: VK (VKontakte) Platform: Focusing on developing and launching VK Mini Apps (HTML5 games), leveraging its vast social user base. RuStore (Russian Android App Store): Publishing and distributing our native client mobile games.
